# Business Model: Forge Shop Scheduling Coordination Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-7221`
- ISCO-08: `7221`
- Occupation: Blacksmiths, Hammersmiths and Forging Press Workers
- Social impact: worker-safety, product-quality, manufacturing-continuity

## Customer

- forge shops / forging contractors
- independent blacksmithing and forging-press crews

## Offer

- crew shift/task scheduling coordination
- task/materials-usage/progress-record logging
- forging-materials supply-order coordination
- safety-concern surfacing to forge-shop safety officers

## Revenue

- monthly retainer
- per-crew coordination fee

## Trust Controls

- no direct finalization of a forging-execution decision (e.g. a
  specific hammer-strike or press-stroke), ever
- no override of a forge-shop safety officer's judgment, ever
- flagged safety concerns (heat-exposure risk, press-crush risk,
  equipment-condition risk) always route to human sign-off, regardless
  of confidence
- no supply order above the registered cost threshold without
  governor-gated human sign-off
- operating and coordination records are auditable, not editable
